Elisia Poelman, born in Geraardsbergen on february 19th 1997, completed her secondary education in visual arts at the Sint Lucas Kunsthumaniora in Ghent. In 2015, Elisia was admitted into LUCA School of Arts Ghent, where she is now working to obtain her bachelor's degree in painting.
Being the young painter that she is, her work is characterized by a constant search, a search where she attempts to translate images and impressions to works of art. 
These images range from the memories that she has made to the ordinary sights people encounter in daily life that may go unnoticed to many, but are of interest to the artist because of what lies below it's surface.
Recently, Elisia has started to explore her childhood memories and is attempting to translate these memories to canvas.
The concept of memory is a source of fascination and inspiration to the artist. How do memories change over time? Are old memories alot more vivid than one would think, or does time erode these memories? These are the questions that drive Elisia, and continue to be an inspiration in her search.
